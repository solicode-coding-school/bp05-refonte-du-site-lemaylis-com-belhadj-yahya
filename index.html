Le Maylis

Pour les amoureux de la nature et tranquillité, visitez-nous.

The lodge
Le Maylis, a rural gîte classified 1st category, is located 25 km
from Tangier in the direction of Asilah. Leaning on the southern
flank of Jebel Zhirou, it offers a magnificent view of the foothills
of the Rif.

Therese welcomes you to this green setting, in the heart of the
countryside, where tranquility and comfort ensure you a relaxing
stay.

Built on a plot of 5000 m2, the cottage has 6 double bedrooms
and can accommodate up to 12 people.

The site
The main building and the bungalows are arranged in a
large garden surrounded by prickly pear pears and a wild
olive grove.

The numerous common areas make the gîte a real living
space where you can share convivial moments with family or friends.
